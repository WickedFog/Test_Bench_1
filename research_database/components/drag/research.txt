==== DRAG LAUNCH & STAGING LOGIC ====
[Image of a drag race Christmas tree with staging lights]
- Concept: Automated, timed staging sequences using power locks to hold the vehicle at specific throttle percentages for tire warmup and start line positioning.
- Drive Modes:
    - Launch (FM0): Default mode providing full throttle pass-through once the sequence completes.
    - Stage (FM1): Restricted throttle (15-25%) for precise start line positioning.
    - WarmUp (FM2): Capped throttle (~70%) for burnout and tire preparation.

==== STAGING SEQUENCE & MIXER IMPLEMENTATION ====
- Logic: Uses dual FUNC_STICKY timers (L02, L03) to create an automated "bump" into the staging beams.
- Stage 1 (Pre-Stage): L02 fires immediately for a 1.0s pulse. Throttle is capped at 25% via a REPL (Replace) mixer to prevent blowing the tires off in the water box.
- Stage 2 (Staged): L03 is delayed by 1.0s, then fires for a 1.0s pulse. Throttle is capped at 75% via a REPL mixer.
- Ramping/Fade: Implementation uses "Slow Up/Down" (typically 1.0s) on staging and launch mixers to create a realistic engine rev, simulating torque climb.

==== LOGICAL SWITCH STRUCTURES ====
- Staging Detection (L01): A FUNC_VPOS gate triggers when the throttle is above a minimum threshold (Throttle > 5) AND the staging enabler switch (SC) is held.
- Timed Pulses (L02/L03): FUNC_STICKY timers create the staging sequence pulses described above.
- Launch Arm Master (L04): A FUNC_STICKY toggle (standardized to SD button) acts as the master flag. Must be active for the launch sequence to fire.

==== SAFETY & FEEDBACK ====
- Instant Kill Switch: If the launch/arm switch is released early (before the green light), the ESC must immediately override CH2 (Throttle) to 0%.
- Visual Christmas Tree: 
    - Flashing Red LED: Not armed or pre-launch staging in progress.
    - Solid Green LED: Sequence complete, launch is armed/ready.
- Audio Assets: Standardized voice tracks mapped to logical switch transitions: "staged.wav", "launch.wav", "burnout.wav".
