==== DRIVE MODE SCALING & RATES ====

- Concept: Global scaling of throttle and steering for specific terrain tasks using Flight Modes (FM).
- Selector: 3-Position Switch SA.
- Logic: Replaces ROLE_THROTTLE and ROLE_STEERING weights based on the active Flight Mode (FM) index.

==== MODE DEFINITIONS ====
- Normal (FM0): Standard trail and bashing use.
    - Rates: 100% Weight / 0% Expo on all primary roles.
- Gate/Crawl (FM1): Precision technical mode for gate-clearing.
    - Throttle: 60% Weight, 30% Expo for refined, low-end startup control.
    - Steering: 70% Weight, 20% Expo to reduce twitchiness at high torque.
- Creep (FM2): Ultra-low speed technical mode for vertical climbs.
    - Throttle: 25% Weight, 45% Expo. Provides maximum trigger resolution at minimum RPM.

==== UI & HARDWARE FEEDBACK ====
- Dashboard Zone 1 (Top Left): Displays the active Flight Mode name on the MT12 screen.
- Audio: Voice prompts "trail_mode.wav", "gate_mode.wav", "crawl_mode.wav" assigned directly to the SA switch positions.
