==== SCALE SIGNALING LOGIC ====

- Decel Brake Lights (Advanced): 
    - Logic: L01 = (TH < 0) AND (dTH/dt > 0). 
    - Effect: Uses the rate of throttle change to illuminate brake LEDs during active "drag braking" (coasting) deceleration, even if the trigger is not physically pulled. Essential for crawler realism.
- Reverse Protection: 
    - Logic: L02 = TH < -10 with a 0.5s Delay.
    - Effect: Prevents the white reverse lights from flickering during rapid forward/reverse transitions or technical "rock rocking."
- Turn Signals: 
    - Logic: L03/L04 trigger when ROLE_STEERING > |80%| for > 1.0s.

==== TELEMETRY RGB STATUS & CONTROL ====
- Headlights: Main lights toggled via SC momentary switch (Sticky L5).
- Solid Red: System Armed / Hot.
- Yellow Flash: Battery voltage critical (Telemetry trigger < 3.4v/cell).
- Blue Flash: Winch-to-Throttle mode (Scherer Swap) is active.
