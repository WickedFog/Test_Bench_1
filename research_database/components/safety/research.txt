==== UNIVERSAL SAFETY INTERLOCKS ====
- Concept: Multi-stage arming and throttle protection standardized for the MT12 ecosystem.
- Throttle Cut (Kill Switch): 
    - Trigger: L01 = Sticky(SD Button). 
    - Action: Immediate master throttle cut overriding CH2 (Drive ESC) and CH3 (Rear/Dig ESC) to 0% (or -100% depending on ESC type).
- Neutral Safety Gate: 
    - Logic: Prevents critical mode swaps (Winch/Drive) or Launch Sequences unless |TH| < 2.
- UI & Status Feedback: 
    - Armed State: RGB LED Solid Red (Hot).
    - Safe State: RGB LED Solid Green (Safe).
    - Audio: "armed.wav" on activation, "disarmed.wav" on cut.
