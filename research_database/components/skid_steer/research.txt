==== DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE (SKID-STEER) ====

- Concept: Control two independent ESCs (Left/Right) using standard Trigger (Throttle) and Wheel (Steering) inputs.
- Hardware Applications: Tanks, Tracked Loaders, Excavators, Dual-Motor Boats (Tugs), and 6x6/8x8 Wheeled Rigs without steering rack geometry.

==== MIXER ARCHITECTURE ====
- Mixer Pattern (Elevon Style): 
    - Left Track (CH1): ROLE_THROTTLE + ROLE_STEERING (Weight: 100/100). (Add)
    - Right Track (CH2): ROLE_THROTTLE - ROLE_STEERING (Weight: 100/100). (Subtract)
- Bias Tuning: Uses a trim switch to adjust Left/Right motor mismatch via a Global Variable.

==== BEHAVIORAL LOGIC ====
- Straight Drive: Both motors sync to full forward/reverse on ROLE_THROTTLE when steering is neutral.
- Pivot Turns: Full opposing counter-rotation occurs when ROLE_THROTTLE is at 0 and ROLE_STEERING is moved.
- Mixed Turn: During forward movement, Steering biases one motor higher/lower to arc.
- Scale Steering Dynamics: Reducing steering weights to 50% in the mixer creates slower pivot turns, simulating more realistic, heavy vehicle inertia.
