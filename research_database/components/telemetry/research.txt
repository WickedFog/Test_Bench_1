==== TELEMETRY MONITORING & ALERTS ====
- Concept: Automated monitoring of vehicle health via ESC and RX sensor data.
- Core Sensors:
    - RxBt: Receiver battery voltage (Internal).
    - Batt: Main drive pack voltage (via S.Port or ESC Telemetry).
    - Temp: ESC temperature (Critical for high-geared bashers/drifters).
    - RPM: Motor speed (Used for scale speed LUA displays).

==== CRITICAL ALERT THRESHOLDS ====
- Low Battery: Threshold < 3.4v per cell.
    - Trigger: L04 (Logical Switch).
    - Action: PlayTrack "lowbatt.wav" + RGB LED Flash (Yellow).
- Overheat: Threshold > 85°C.
    - Trigger: L05 (Logical Switch).
    - Action: PlayTrack "overheat.wav" + Override TH to 50% (Thermal Limp Mode).

==== LUA INTEGRATION ====
- Data Passthrough: Telemetry values are routed to the "Winch Panel" or "Dashboard" LUA scripts to provide real-time visual feedback on the MT12 screen.
